## STRIDE Threat Register

| Threat ID | Category | Component | Severity | Disposition | Mitigation Plan |
|-----------|----------|-----------|----------|-------------|-----------------|
| T-72-03-01 | Tampering/Data loss | `SaveAsync` blank-secret handling | high | mitigate | A blank secret skips the secret upsert entirely (never overwrites with empty), unit-tested — prevents silently wiping a working SSO credential on an unrelated edit. |
| T-72-03-02 | Information Disclosure | Weak secret generation | medium | mitigate | `RandomNumberGenerator.GetHexString(64)` (CSPRNG, 256-bit) — never `Guid`/`System.Random`; a predictable secret would let an attacker forge SSO tokens. |
| T-72-03-03 | Elevation of Privilege | Cross-scope leakage in the cascade | medium | mitigate | `GetForScopeAsync`/`GetCascadeValueAsync` filter strictly on the exact `GroupId`; a group override never bleeds into another group and the default only applies as an explicit fallback. |
| T-72-03-SC | Tampering | NuGet package installs | low | accept | Zero new packages; all APIs are BCL/already-referenced. |
</threat_model>

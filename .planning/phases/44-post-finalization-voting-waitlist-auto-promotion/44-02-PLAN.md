<objective>
Add the promotion orchestration and the single-recipient email pipeline. `QuestService` gains a `ChangeVoteAsync` and a `RevokeSignupAsync` that both funnel a freed seat through one shared `PromoteNextWaitlistedPlayerIfSeatFreedAsync` path, which finds the top waitlisted candidate, flips them selected, and enqueues a promotion email addressed to exactly that one player. The email reuses the `FinalizedEmail` Razor/HtmlRenderer/Hangfire/Resend pipeline verbatim, with a new `WaitlistPromoted.razor` template and a `QuestWaitlistPromotedEmailJob`.

Purpose: VOTE-04 (seat frees → promote), VOTE-05 (Maybe keeps seat, no promote), and VOTE-07 (email goes only to the promoted player). Per D-04, both actions stay available side by side — "Revoke Signup" remains a hard delete and "Vote No" is the softer record-retaining action, and BOTH must funnel a freed seat through the same promotion method so neither call site can silently omit the check. D-06 governs the email: reuse the `FinalizedEmail` visual style/pipeline, new copy only.
Output: `QuestService.ChangeVoteAsync`/`RevokeSignupAsync`/`PromoteNextWaitlistedPlayerIfSeatFreedAsync`, `EnqueueWaitlistPromotedEmail` (interface + Hangfire impl), `QuestWaitlistPromotedEmailJob`, `WaitlistPromoted.razor`, and extended `QuestServiceTests` covering VOTE-04/05/07.
</objective>

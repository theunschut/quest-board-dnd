<objective>
Migrate the 6 Platform-area alert-banner views (Group Index/Members, Users Index — desktop + mobile) off their local `alert alert-dismissible` flash markup onto the shared toast partial (D-08). These views resolve to `_Layout.Platform.cshtml`/`_Layout.Platform.Mobile.cshtml`, which were wired with `<partial name="_Toasts" />` in Plan 01 — so removing the local banners leaves the toast partial to render the same TempData messages as toasts.

Purpose: Eliminate the duplicated alert-banner markup in the Platform area. This is the group of views RESEARCH Critical Discrepancy #1 flagged: they use the separate Platform layout pair, so the Plan 01 Platform-layout wiring is what makes this migration safe (no toast-less regression).

Output: 6 Platform-area views with local alert banners removed.
</objective>

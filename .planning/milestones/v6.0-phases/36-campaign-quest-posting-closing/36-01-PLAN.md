<objective>
Establish the persistence + test foundation for the campaign quest Close/Reopen lifecycle: add `IsClosed`/`ClosedDate` to the EF entity and the domain model, generate the EF Core migration, and extend the integration-test seeding helpers so downstream plans can seed campaign-type groups and closed campaign quests.

Purpose: Every downstream plan (service methods, controller actions, views, tests) reads or writes `IsClosed`/`ClosedDate`. This plan creates those fields and the test scaffolding so the rest of the phase has a stable contract to build against. This is the deliberately separate, additive second lifecycle locked by PROJECT.md — `IsClosed`/`ClosedDate` are net-new fields, NOT a reuse of `IsFinalized`/`FinalizedDate`. Keeping `FinalizedDate` untouched is what structurally excludes campaign quests from `DailyReminderJob` and `QuestFinalizedEmailJob` (CQUEST-06).
Output: Two new nullable/bool entity+domain fields, one reversible EF migration, extended `TestDataHelper` with campaign-group + closed-quest seeding.
</objective>
